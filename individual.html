Individual

As there are lots of fake news, misinformation, disinformation, it is important to understand how to deal with those message properly.

Individually, people can double confirm and fact check the information when they collect the message form others. Thank to the advance of technology and the raise of awareness, there are some tool helping us to check the fact of those information.

Fact Check Tools

  • HKBU Fact Check

    • HKBU Fact Chat is independently operated by the School of Communication, Hong Kong Baptist University.

  • Fact Check Explore

    • The Fact Check Tools consist of two tools: Fact Check Explorer and Fact Check Markup Tool. Both tools aim to facilitate the work of fact checkers, journalists and researchers. Google does not endorse or create any of these fact checks. If you disagree with one, please contact the website owner that published it.

  • Factcheck Lab

    • The purpose of the "Factcheck Lab" is to use fact-checking methods to enable the public to build a more truthful, kind and beautiful society based on facts. Factcheck Lab refers to the fact-checking standards of the International Fact-Checking Network (IFCN) and operates on the principles of professionalism, transparency, and fairness. It was certified in October 2021 and became a signatory member of the IFCN fact-checking standards. Factcheck Lab is a media literacy education project launched by the Culture and Media Education Fund Limited (CMEF) in May 2020, and directly funded the establishment of the team. CMEF was incorporated in accordance with Article 88 of the Inland Revenue Ordinance of Hong Kong and is a tax-exempt local public charity.

However, the most important and efficient ways to distinguish message is to keep the sence of factchecking.
